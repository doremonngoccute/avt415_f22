User Scenario Mapping

Scenario

Calvin is a college student who has class until 8 p.m. He usually skips his dinner or makes instant noodles when he gets home because he has no time to cook a high-quality meal. One day, Calvin decides to order food online. He found Maki Mart, a store that sells sushi rolls 24 hours a day and can order sushi online for delivery or in person. He decides to order himself a sushi roll for dinner which contains more nutrients than instant noodles and he does not have to cook.

Actor

Calvin

Motivator

Having classes until 8 p.m.

Intention

Calvin wants a quick dinner after class that requires no cooking or preparation but still full of nutrients and protein.

Action

Calvin starts looking online to find a close by restaurant or ones that deliver food. He looks for restaurants with high-quality food that are still open and can pick up food or deliver. Calvin comes across Maki Mart, a food store.

Resolution

He makes an online pick-up order and has a delicous sushi roll for dinner.
